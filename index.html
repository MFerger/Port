Milk Drop

Milk drop was created as a place for over-producting mothers can contact under-producting mothers locally. Users can add a posting which takes the geolocation of your computer and adds a location marker on the map. Here is a link to the live site.

Technologies Used:

Express, Node, JQuery, Mapbox API, SASS, OAuth Integration and Bootstrap

Bracket Maker

Bracket Maker was designed and targeted for people who need an easy way to keep track of tabletop games with friends. This can be used for pool, ping pong, foosball and much more. Users are able to create/join a bracket of any name, add players and determine who is the winner of each match. This is a app for iPhone and Andriod currently currently in the process of being implemented into the App Store and Google Play Store. Here is a link to the live site.

Technologies Used:

AngularJS, Express, Node, JQuery, Ionic Framework, Cardova, Mongo, Mongoose and Bootstrap

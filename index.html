Bracket Maker

Bracket Maker was designed and targeted for people who need an easy way to keep track of tabletop games with friends. This can be used for pool, ping pong, foosball and much more. Users are able to create/join a bracket of any name, add players and determine who is the winner of each match. This is a app for iPhone and Andriod currently currently in the process of being implemented into the App Store and Google Play Store. Here is a link to the live site.

Technologies Used:

AngularJS, Express, Node, JQuery, Ionic Framework, Cardova, Mongo, Mongoose, Pivitol Tracker and Bootstrap

Tesseract

Tesseract was built in 6 hours at a hackathon for people who want a simple elegent timer. This can be used to time and save anything, but is primarily used for solving rubiks cubes. Users can login and save their times, as well as compare to the sites leaderboards against other players. Here is a link to the live site.

Technologies Used:

Express, Node, JQuery, Bootstrap

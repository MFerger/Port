Milk Drop

Milk drop was created as a place for over-producing mothers can contact under-producing mothers locally. Users can add a posting which takes the geolocation of your computer and adds a location marker on the map. Here is a link to the live site.

Technologies Used:

Express, Node, JQuery, Mapbox API, SASS, OAuth Integration, Pivitol Tracker and Bootstrap

Bracket Maker

Bracket Maker was designed and targeted for people who need an easy way to keep track of tabletop games with friends. Users are able to create/join a bracket of any name, add players and determine who is the winner of each match. Here is a link to the codebase.

Technologies Used:

AngularJS, Express, Node, JQuery, Ionic Framework, Cardova, Mongo, Mongoose, Pivitol Tracker and Bootstrap

Tesseract

Tesseract was built in 6 hours at a hackathon for people who want a simple elegent timer. This can be used to time and save anything, but is primarily used for solving rubiks cubes. Users can login and save their times, as well as compare to the sites leaderboards against other players. Here is a link to the live site.

Technologies Used:

Express, Node, JQuery, Bootstrap
